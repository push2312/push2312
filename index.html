Department of Electrical Engineering · IIT Jodhpur

Researcher in resilient control of multi-agent systems, specialising in event-triggered mechanisms, coordination control, and security against adversarial attacks on networked cyber-physical systems.

At the Distributed Systems and Control Lab (DSCLab), IIT Jodhpur, I develop resilient consensus algorithms, attack detection and mitigation strategies, and event-triggered control frameworks validated on ground and aerial robots with motion-capture systems.

Research Interests

Event-Triggered Control

Coordination and control of multi-agent systems under spatio-temporal and geometric constraints, reducing communication and computation overhead.

Resilient Distributed Control

Safe, secure, and resilient distributed control strategies for robotic networks under adversarial conditions and limited information.
